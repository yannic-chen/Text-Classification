New York (CNN Business) Gold prices have soared more than 20% in 2020, and they hit a new record high earlier this year.
But platinum prices are down almost 15% year-to-date.
Investors often flock to gold and other metals in times of financial uncertainty.
One key reason is that it has more of an industrial use than other metals.
Platinum is a key component of catalytic converters in cars, which are used to reduce exhaust emissions.
