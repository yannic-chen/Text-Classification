(Kitco News) - Gold is ending the week on the front foot as the precious metal pushed 0.89% higher overnight.

The New Zealand dollar is the clear outperformer and trades half a percent higher against the greenback overnight.
Later in the session, the market will get the latest employment change data from Canada and this weekend Trump is set to hold a Republican rally.
