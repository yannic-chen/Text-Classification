The U.S. dollar is seeing some strength as markets are pricing in a lesser chance for additional fiscal stimulus to be passed before the election, which is weighing on gold, according to Commerzbank.
Gold's longer-term outlook remains positive as more stimulus is likely to be passed after the election.
