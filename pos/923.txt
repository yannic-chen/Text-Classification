Gold May Have A Good Chance To Rebound After Recent Sell-OffThe main victim of the recent market optimism was the precious metal segment.
Gold and silver were under significant pressure as traders sold safe haven assets.
This is a setup for a weaker U.S. dollar and strong gold.
Oil-related stocks enjoyed a very strong rebound in November and pullback risks are high unless oil manages to continue its rally.
Today, oil is moving lower in a thin market, but the real test is due next week when all traders return to their desks after holidays.
