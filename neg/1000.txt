 The ongoing Bitcoin (BTC) rally has primarily been driven by institutions, analysts say, with metrics such as CME’s open interest and Grayscale’s assets under management (AUM), supporting this narrative.
At the same time, the gold market has seen large outflows in recent weeks.
On Nov. 24, independent financial researcher Jan Nieuwenhuijs reported that gold saw its largest weekly outflow in history.
pic.twitter.com/Re4o3PHrel — Jan Nieuwenhuijs (@JanGold_) November 23, 2020The timing of the heightened level of outflows from the gold market is noteworthy because it comes after the entrance of major institutional investors into the Bitcoin market.
Some long-time Bitcoin investors, who had gold positions for prolonged periods, have also started to allocate their capital fully into BTC.
