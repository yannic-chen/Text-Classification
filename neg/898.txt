Gold (XAU/USD) has broken below the previous support level of $1,850 per ounce.
Georgette Boele, Senior Precious Metals Strategist at ABN Amro, remarks that the long-term uptrend would be over if the yellow metal losses the $1,800 level.
Key quotes“The 200-day moving average comes in just above the psychological level of $1,800 per ounce.
We expect a test of this 200-day moving average.
If prices break below and also close below, we think that the long-term uptrend is over.”“We are cautious as many investors have bought gold and they are getting more nervous.
