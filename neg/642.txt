Gold futures logged their first loss in four sessions on Tuesday.
Prices eased back from recent gains after failing to breach the key $1,900-an-ounce mark, even as benchmark U.S. stock indexes traded broadly lower and the dollar weakened.
December gold GCZ20, -0.12% edged down by $2.70, or 0.1%, to settle at $1,885.10 an ounce.
