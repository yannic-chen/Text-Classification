According to a new report by the World Gold Council, demand for gold in Q3 2020 – 2,972 tonnes year to date – demand is 10% below the same period in 2019.
Q3 demand was 892.3 tonnes – the lowest quarterly total since Q3 2009.
Gold demand for jewellery at 333 tonnes was 29% below a slow Q3 2019.
