XAU/USD accelerates its downtrend to hit five-month lows at $1,775Gold suffers amid the positive market sentiment.
Breach of $1,800 support increases Bullion's negative momentum.
Gold futures accelerated heir downtrend from last week highs near $1,900, breaking below the 200-day SMA, at $1,800 area, to hit its lowest prices in nearly five months, at $1,775.
Bullion prices are attempting to regain lost ground on the late US session although they still remain well below the key $1,800 support area.
With technical indicators on the daily chart already into oversold levels, the XAU/USD might bounce up before dropping further.
