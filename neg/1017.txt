Risk tone remains positive amid vaccine hopes, mixed clues over Brexit deal.
China’s official PMI can offer immediate direction, risk catalysts to remain as the key driver.
Gold prices recede to $1,786, down 0.10% intraday, during the early Asian trading on Monday.
The reason could mainly be traced to the coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccine hopes while mixed clues concerning the Brexit trade deal offer extra direction to the bullion traders.
If the key activity data from the world’s second-largest economy remains firm, gold has more room on the downside.
