Now, his hedge fund is talking up Bitcoin, which should only help it as it looks to lure investors.
Scaramucci’s hedge fund, SkyBridge Capital, which started trading on December 22 with $25.3 million in Bitcoin, has officially opened its Bitcoin fund to accredited investors.
(According to Scaramucci, the hedge fund as a whole has over $300 million of funds in the BTC market.)
The SkyBridge Bitcoin Fund gives accredited US investors—those with regular annual incomes of at least $200,000—exposure to Bitcoin in exchange for a management fee of 0.75%; the minimum investment is $50,000.
