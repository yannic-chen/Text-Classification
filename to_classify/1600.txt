 A confluence of factors are affecting gold prices, including Bitcoin’s upward run, rising Treasury yields, and a stronger dollar.
This is feeding into heavy daily volume for the VanEck Vectors Gold Miners ETF (GDX) and VanEck Vectors Junior Gold Miners ETF (GDXJ).
GDXJ seeks to replicate as closely as possible the price and yield performance of the MVIS® Global Junior Gold Miners Index.
“Gold is highly revered for its great returns and resilience during economic downturns, but during gold bull markets there’s something that regularly provides even greater returns: the ownership of gold mining stocks,” a Visual Capitalist article points out.
“As a gold mining company mines and produces gold, the gold is sold on the market fairly quickly to avoid the risk of gold’s price depreciating,” the article noted further.
