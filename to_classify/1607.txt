 TORONTO, Jan. 19, 2021 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- E2Gold Inc. (TSXV: ETU) (the “Company” or “E2Gold”) is pleased to report that it has completed a large helicopter-borne magnetic survey on its Hawkins gold project in northern Ontario.
The Support Agreement has an initial term expiring on April 19, 2021, following which it will continue on a month-to-month basis until terminated by the Company upon the provision of written notice.
The Support Agreement remains subject to the approval of the TSXV.
Its large flagship property, the Hawkins Gold Project, covers 7 townships in north-central Ontario, about 140 km east of Hemlo.
