 The sweeping stimulus packages doled out by major central banks and governments pushed gold prices to a record high.
As we take a stride forward to the year 2021, we reckon that the fundamental drivers anchoring gold prices on a higher trajectory remain in place.
Talking about the investment demand, gold ETFs witnessed net outflows in November, for the first time in 12 months.
Even in case of any adverse move, prices look to find a floor at $1,680 an ounce or Rs 44,800 per 10 gm.
From a yearly perspective, prices can even spur higher towards Rs 65,000 per 10 gm mark.
